# Regulatory Boundary Memo

This is an internal product-boundary memo, not legal advice.

MacMarket-Trader's current scope is research and paper trading workflow
support only.

## Current Boundary

- MacMarket-Trader is research/paper-only in current scope.
- It does not perform live trading.
- It does not provide broker routing.
- It does not automate exits.
- It does not exercise discretionary management over user assets.
- It does not collect client suitability information for fiduciary advice.
- It does not manage assets under management.
- It is not offered as public investment advice without legal review.
- It is not a broker-dealer, registered investment adviser, or exchange.

## Advice Boundary

Recommendations are internal research artifacts for operator review. They are
not personalized fiduciary advice. Future public, commercial, client-facing,
or asset-management use requires securities counsel review before launch.

## LLM Boundary

LLMs explain, summarize, and compare only. Deterministic systems own approval,
side, entry, stop, target, sizing, risk gates, order creation, and paper
position review classifications.

## Required Review Before Expansion

- Securities counsel review.
- Broker/routing architecture review.
- Suitability/adviser boundary review.
- Marketing and disclosure review.
- Privacy and vendor contract review.
