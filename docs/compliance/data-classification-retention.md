# Data Classification And Retention

| data class | classification | retention target | deletion/reset behavior | export/backup notes | privacy concern | current implementation/gap |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| User identity/profile | Confidential | Active account lifetime plus 1 year after removal unless legal need differs. | Admin user delete/suspend paths exist; formal deletion procedure needed. | Included in DB backups. | Email/name tie activity to a person. | Local app DB owns approval/role; export/deletion workflow gap. |
| Auth identifiers | Confidential | Active account lifetime plus 1 year. | Deleted with local user record where implemented; Clerk deletion is external. | Included in DB backups. | External auth ID maps to Clerk account. | Reconciliation exists; formal offboarding checklist needed. |
| Recommendations | Internal confidential | 3 years during alpha unless purged for test/demo cleanup. | Paper reset does not delete recommendations. | Included in DB backups and release evidence should not include full payloads unless needed. | May reveal strategy logic and operator notes. | Retention policy documented; automated retention gap. |
| Paper orders/fills/positions/trades | Internal confidential | 3 years for audit/replay evidence during alpha. | `/user/paper/reset` deletes current user's equity paper records only. | Included in DB backups. | Paper-only but may reveal user behavior. | Lifecycle/reset tested; retention automation gap. |
| Replay runs/steps | Internal confidential | 3 years for validation evidence. | Not deleted by paper reset. | Included in DB backups. | May include strategy decisions and event text. | Retention automation gap. |
| Audit logs | Restricted internal | 5 years target for security/admin/workflow actions. | Not deleted by paper reset. | Backup and export should preserve integrity. | May include emails, action metadata, and workflow payloads. | Need tamper-evidence/export process. |
| Provider-health logs | Internal | 1 year summary; shorter raw error retention if noisy. | Manual log rotation currently. | Release evidence captures sanitized config/probe state. | Provider error messages might contain metadata. | Redaction tests exist; formal log retention gap. |
| LLM prompts/responses/provenance | Confidential | 1 year during alpha, or shorter if legal/privacy review requires. | No dedicated deletion path yet. | Avoid full prompt dumps in release evidence. | User/event text can enter vendor systems. | Explanation-only boundary exists; retention/deletion gap. |
| Email/invite logs | Confidential | 3 years for onboarding/audit during alpha. | Invite revoke/delete path exists; email logs remain. | Included in DB backups. | Email addresses and invite state. | Invite tokens now masked in admin payloads; retention gap. |
| Settings/watchlists | Confidential | Active account lifetime plus 1 year. | User/admin deletion needed for full purge; paper reset does not delete. | Included in DB backups. | Symbols may reveal interests/research. | Current-user scoped; retention gap. |
| Local logs | Internal/Confidential depending content | 30-90 days target. | Excluded from release archives/deploy mirror; rotate manually. | Do not include in shareable archives by default. | Could contain errors, emails, paths, provider metadata. | Need automated log rotation. |
| Screenshots/smoke artifacts | Internal | 90 days or per release evidence need. | Stored under ignored local evidence/test folders. | Excluded from clean release archive. | Screenshots may show user data. | Manual retention and redaction review needed. |
